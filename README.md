POTUX
The Universal Bridge: Fusing Linux & Unix
POTUX is just an experimental hobby project. The goal is simple: to explore a fusion between the flexibility of the Linux kernel and the rigid architecture of Unix standards.
It is a work in progress, built with curiosity rather than grand ambitions. I am just a student of these systems, trying to see how they can play together.

The world of operating systems is divided between the strict requirements of copyleft and the freedom of permissive licenses. POTUX sits in the middle, purely as a technical experiment to bridge these two paradigms. It is not an attempt to redefine the industry, but rather a personal journey to understand how systems are built.
